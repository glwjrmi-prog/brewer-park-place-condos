    Community Rules

    Searchable rules and regulations

    Click here for complete Rules & Regulations

    General

    No yard sales, garage sales, rummage or estate sales, or auctions are permitted.

    Co-owners selling units must notify the Board and provide realtor information.

    Exterior of Units and Common Areas

    No more than three security cameras may be attached to a unit.

    Wind chimes are prohibited.

    Trash containers must remain in garages except on trash day.

    Propane fire tables are allowed if compliant with fire codes.

    Parking

    Guests should use driveways or guest parking lots.

    No parking on sidewalks or grass.

    Garage doors should remain closed except during active use.

    Pets

    Pets must be leashed and under control outdoors.

    Residents must immediately clean up after pets.

    Pet owners must complete pet registration.

    Violations and Fines

    The Board may levy fines for violations.

    $100 fines may apply after 30 days.

    $500 fines may apply after 60 days or repeated violations.
